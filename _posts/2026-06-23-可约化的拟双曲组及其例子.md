### 可约化的拟双曲组的柯西问题

#### 一般特征的情形

考虑如下带初始条件的拟双曲组的柯西问题：
$$
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
&\left\{\begin{aligned}
&\frac{\partial r}{\partial t}+\lambda(r,s)\frac{\partial r}{\partial x}=0,\\
&\frac{\partial s}{\partial t}+\mu(r,s)\frac{\partial s}{\partial x}=0.
\end{aligned}\right.\\
&t=0:~r(x,0)=r_0(x),~s(x,0)=s_0(x).
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
